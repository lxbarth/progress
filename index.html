Frame your MapBox map

  • 1. Click 'Embed' on your map in the MapBox hosting account
    The Embed button is the first one on the Info tab of your map.
  • 2. Look for the map ID in the TileJSON URL
    Example: http://a.tiles.mapbox.com/v3/lxbarth.map-v9o1vc83.jsonp
  • 3. Go to http://lxbarth.github.com/?[map-id]
    Example: http://lxbarth.github.com/frame?lxbarth.map-v9o1vc83
